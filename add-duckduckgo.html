Take Back Your Privacy! Set DuckDuckGo as your default search engine

1

Click the ellipsis icon in the toolbar.

2

Click Settings in the dropdown.

3

Click Privacy and Services in the left sidebar.

4

Scroll down to the Services section and click Address bar.

5

Click the dropdown menu beside Search engine used in the address bar and select DuckDuckGo.

Stop worrying, start browsing!
